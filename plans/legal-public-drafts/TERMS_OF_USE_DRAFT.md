## 3. Accounts

Users may browse the public map without an account.

Some features require a Firebase account, such as saved visited places, saved routes, profile/account features, Premium checkout, and Premium-only server features.

Email/password users may need to verify their email address before using checkout or Premium server features. Google sign-in users are handled through Firebase Auth.

Users are responsible for:

- Providing accurate account information.
- Keeping login credentials secure.
- Using the same account for checkout and Premium access.
- Not sharing access in a way that bypasses product limits.

## 4. Public Catalog And Map Information

The app shows park, program, route, and map information from public catalog data, Google Sheets/static CSV data, user-facing app data, and third-party map providers.

This information may be incomplete, outdated, inaccurate, or changed by parks or third parties without notice. Users should verify rules, closures, fees, pet policies, safety conditions, and accessibility directly with the park or official source before traveling.

The app should not promise perfect GPS, route, park-rule, or location accuracy.

## 5. User Content And Saved Data

Users may save visited places, routes, trip notes, feedback, or other account data.

Users should not upload or submit unlawful, abusive, misleading, infringing, private, or sensitive information. Users should not submit passwords, payment card details, or confidential information through feedback/support forms.

Final terms should define any license needed for user-submitted content and support messages.

## 6. Free And Premium Features

The app may offer free features and paid Premium features. Free features may have limits, such as saved visited-place limits or disabled Premium tools.

Do not describe Premium as "unlimited." Any route/geocode, saved-route, leaderboard, or usage feature may be subject to reasonable technical, abuse-prevention, cost, or safety limits.

Premium access is server-authoritative and is based on account entitlement state.

## 7. Lemon Squeezy Checkout, Subscriptions, And Coupons

Paid checkout and subscription handling are provided by Lemon Squeezy. Users enter coupon codes on Lemon Squeezy's secure checkout page.

The app does not grant Premium from an app-side coupon field. Premium activates only after Lemon Squeezy checkout/subscription confirmation is received through server-side webhook processing.

Coupon/discount codes, including 100% off admin/mod/VIP/support codes, are Lemon Squeezy discount codes. Coupon terms may include expiration dates, usage limits, product/variant limits, duration limits, and renewal behavior.

Admin/mod/VIP/support 100% off coupons should not be described as creating Firebase admin permissions. They only affect Premium subscription price/access according to Lemon Squeezy discount behavior and webhook confirmation.

## 8. Renewals, Cancellations, And Refunds
