# Controlled Release Tester Script

Date: 2026-05-09
Audience: 25-50 controlled beta testers.
Status: tester instructions. Lemon Squeezy remains in test mode.

## Tester Mission

Thank you for helping test BARK Ranger Map. Please use it like a real trip-planning and park-tracking tool, then report anything confusing, broken, slow, or surprising.

Do not enter real payment information unless Carter gives you separate instructions. Premium checkout is still in Lemon Squeezy test mode.

## What To Test

1. Open the app on your normal device.
2. Browse the public map signed out.
3. Search for a park or town using local search.
4. Try filters.
5. Create or sign into an account.
6. If using email/password, check for the verification email.
7. Verify your email if prompted.
8. Refresh account status after verifying.
9. Mark 5 parks as visited.
10. Try to mark a 6th park as a free user.
11. Confirm the app explains that more than 5 tracked parks is Premium.
12. Open the Premium/paywall screen.
13. Open Lemon test checkout, but do not use real payment unless Carter asks.
14. If Carter gives you a Lemon test coupon, enter it on Lemon checkout.
15. Try the route planner.
16. Try premium route generation if your account has Premium/test entitlement.
17. Try global town/city search if your account has Premium/test entitlement.
18. Check the leaderboard.
19. Open your account/billing page.
20. Try the app on a phone or mobile-width browser.
21. Submit feedback from a signed-in account.

## What To Report

Please send Carter:

- device type,
- browser,
- account email used,
- screenshot or screen recording if possible,
- exact action you took,
- what you expected,
- what happened instead,
- time the issue happened,
- whether refreshing fixed it.

Example:

> iPhone 15, Safari, tester@example.com. I searched "Yosemite", tapped Mark Visited for my 6th park, expected Premium prompt, but the button spun forever. Screenshot attached. Happened around 8:15 PM ET.

## Important Notes

- The app is still in beta.
- Browsing the map is free.
- Free accounts can track 5 visited parks.
- More tracked parks, saved routes, route generation, and deeper Premium tools are gated.
- Lemon checkout is test mode unless Carter tells you otherwise.
- Coupon codes are entered on Lemon checkout, not inside the app.
- Some cosmetic badges/achievements may change later.

## Stop Testing And Tell Carter Immediately If

- the app is blank,
- you can see another tester's account data,
- checkout asks for real payment unexpectedly,
- Premium appears/disappears incorrectly,
- you cannot sign out,
- the app repeatedly crashes,
- route/search actions seem to run over and over.
