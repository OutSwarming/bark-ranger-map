# Legal Review Open Questions

Status: **Draft pending legal review.**

This is not legal advice. These are open questions for Carter and counsel before paid/public release.

## Operator And Ownership

1. What legal entity operates BARK Ranger Map?
2. Who owns the app code, app design, brand assets, catalog data, user accounts, and customer relationship?
3. Who owns or controls the Lemon Squeezy store and payouts?
4. Who is responsible for refunds, chargebacks, taxes, support, and privacy requests?
5. What happens to app/customer/payment/data ownership if a partner/admin/community relationship changes or ends?

## Brand, Trademark, And Affiliation

1. Can the app use BARK Ranger, US BARK RANGERS, B.A.R.K., and related marks/logos in a paid product?
2. Who owns each logo, watermark, app icon, and brand asset?
3. Is any public-agency, park, NPS, or third-party permission needed?
4. What exact public language should describe brand ownership?
5. What language should avoid implying official endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation?

## Privacy Policy

1. What final privacy policy is required for Firebase Auth emails, Google sign-in, email verification, visited places, saved routes, feedback, analytics, payment metadata, and support messages?
2. What user data is considered personal, sensitive, location-like, or protected?
3. What retention periods should apply?
4. What export/deletion process and response timeframe should be promised?
5. Are analytics consent or opt-out requirements triggered by GoatCounter or other third-party browser requests?
6. Does Google Fonts/CDN loading require disclosure or consent in target jurisdictions?

## Payments, Subscriptions, Refunds, And Coupons

1. What refund window and eligibility rules should Carter offer?
2. What cancellation language is required for annual subscriptions?
3. How should renewal and auto-renew language appear in checkout and account UI?
4. Are 100% off Lemon coupons for admins/mods/VIPs/support users okay from a tax/accounting/consumer-disclosure perspective?
5. Should admin/mod free access use forever discounts only to avoid surprise renewal charges?
6. How should one-year-only 100% off discounts warn users about possible future renewal at full price?
7. Does Lemon Squeezy merchant-of-record status change any policy wording?

## Catalog Data And User Safety

1. Who owns the Google Sheet and local CSV/JSON snapshots?
2. Were park/pin records copied from public websites, maps, Facebook, user submissions, AllTrails, park pages, or other sources?
3. Are source links/attributions needed at row level or app level?
4. Can the app cache and redistribute the Google Sheets CSV data through static fallback files?
5. What disclaimers are needed for pet rules, closures, accessibility, fees, park conditions, and safety?
6. What language should avoid overpromising route accuracy, GPS verification, or park-rule accuracy?

## Third-Party Services And Attribution

1. Are current Leaflet, OpenStreetMap, OpenTopoMap, Esri, and OpenRouteService attributions sufficient?
2. Are public OSM/OpenTopoMap/Esri tile endpoints appropriate for expected public/paid traffic?
3. Does ORS require specific attribution near route/geocode features?
4. Should the app move to a paid/commercial map tile provider before public launch?
5. What notices are required for Firebase/Google, Lemon Squeezy, GoatCounter, Google Fonts, and browser CDNs?

## Terms Of Use

1. What liability limitations, warranty disclaimers, and acceptable-use terms are enforceable for the operator's jurisdiction?
2. Should there be age limits or parental-consent language?
3. What user-content license is needed for feedback, data corrections, saved routes, and user-entered notes?
4. What anti-abuse terms are needed for route/geocode rate limits, leaderboard integrity, scraping, and direct Firebase access attempts?
5. What termination/suspension language is appropriate?

## Support And Operational Process

1. What support email should be public?
2. What information can support request from users?
3. What information should users be told not to send?
4. Who has admin access to Firebase, Lemon Squeezy, Google Sheets, and support inboxes?
5. What internal process handles data deletion, refunds, entitlement repair, and billing disputes?

## Repository / Public Docs

1. Should legal drafts live in the public repository or private-only docs?
2. Should the repo remain public before legal review is complete?
3. Should old legal/QC/debug history be removed or should the repo be made private?
4. What public README, license, notices, privacy, terms, and support links should exist before paid launch?

This document should remain internal until Carter and counsel decide what public language to publish.
