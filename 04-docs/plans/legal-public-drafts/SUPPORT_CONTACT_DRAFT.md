## How To Contact Support

Email: **[support email TBD]**

Suggested subject lines:

- Account help
- Premium checkout help
- Lemon Squeezy billing help
- Coupon help
- Park data correction
- Route or map issue
- Data deletion/export request

## What To Include

For faster help, include:

- The email address used for your BARK Ranger Map account.
- The device and browser you are using.
- What you were trying to do.
- What happened instead.
- Any error message you saw.
- Approximate date/time of the issue.
- For checkout issues, the Lemon Squeezy receipt/order email if different from your app account email.

Do not send:

- Passwords.
- Full payment card numbers.
- Sensitive personal information that is not needed for support.

## Premium Or Coupon Help

All coupon codes are entered at Lemon Squeezy checkout. BARK Ranger Map does not have an app-side coupon/access-code box.

Premium activates only after Lemon Squeezy checkout/subscription confirmation is processed. If Premium does not appear after checkout, contact support with the app account email and Lemon receipt/order email.

## Cancellation Or Refund Help

If a Lemon subscription exists, users should use the Lemon Squeezy billing/customer portal when available. Support can help locate the correct account or investigate subscription status.

Refund requests should include the app account email, Lemon receipt/order email, purchase date, and a short explanation.

## Data Deletion Or Export Requests

To request account data export or deletion:

1. Email **[support email TBD]** from the email associated with the account.
2. Include "Data request" in the subject.
3. State whether you want export, deletion, correction, or another request.
4. Support may need to verify account ownership before processing.

Some payment, tax, security, fraud-prevention, or backup records may need to be retained where legally required.

## Park Data Corrections

Park rules, pet policies, closures, program availability, fees, and operating details can change. Users should verify current information directly with the park or official source before traveling.

To report an app data issue, include:

- Park/place name.
- The correction requested.
- A link to a source if available.
- Date you checked the information.

## Safety Reminder

BARK Ranger Map is a planning and tracking tool. It does not replace official park rules, emergency services, weather alerts, trail conditions, or local safety guidance.
